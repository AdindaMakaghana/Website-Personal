Ode to the Ocean

Oh mighty ocean, vast and deep, Your secrets hidden, your mysteries keep. In shades of blue, you ebb and flow, A timeless dance, a rhythmic show.
Beneath your waves, a world unknown, Where creatures roam, in realms alone. From tiny krill to giants grand, In your embrace, they make their stand.
Your waves they crash upon the shore, A timeless song forevermore. With every tide, a story told, Of journeys ventured, of tales untold.
Your salty breeze, it fills the air, A whispered voice, beyond compare. It calls to sailors, bold and free, To set their sails and brave the sea.
Oh ocean deep, you hold such power, A force of nature, in every hour. Yet in your depths, a tranquil peace, A sanctuary where worries cease.
So here I stand, upon your shore, In awe of all you have in store. Oh mighty ocean, wild and free, You'll forever be a part of me.
